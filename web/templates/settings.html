About

S3 Usage Monitor polls your configured S3 accounts and tracks storage usage against quotas.

  • Account checks run automatically based on the configured interval.
  • You can also trigger a manual check from the Dashboard.
  • Telegram notifications are sent per-account when the usage threshold is crossed.
